Life Should be Easier...

And it can be. The Peripheral-Framework aims to simplify and streamline the proccess of creating peripherals for Minecraft mods such as ComputerCraft and OpenComputers. By acting as a buffer between unnecessarily complex APIs and your code, this framework will turn your code into a paradigm of simplicity, conciceness, and maintainability.
